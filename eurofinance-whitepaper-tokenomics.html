1. Overview

Project Summary

Euro Finance (ticker: EURC) is a euro‑denominated crypto asset designed for fast, low‑cost transfers and use in decentralized finance (DeFi). The project focuses on providing a simple and transparent infrastructure for cross‑chain payments and on‑chain euro‑based accounting.

This short whitepaper describes the basic concept of the project, key principles, and high‑level tokenomics. It is intentionally compact and may be extended with more detailed technical documentation later.
